### **Responsibilities of HR and Marketing Manager**

---

## **Human Resources (HR) Responsibilities**

* Coordinate with all team members and ensure smooth day-to-day HR operations.
* Manage the **end-to-end hiring process**, including sourcing, screening, interview coordination, and onboarding.
* Work on the **resourcing and hiring plan**, targeting **at least one hire per month (minimum)** based on business needs.
* Prepare, manage, and maintain **offer letters, appointment letters, NDAs, contracts, and freelancer agreements**.
* Track and manage **employee attendance, leaves, overtime, work-from-home records**, and related documentation.
* Coordinate **freelancer and employee payments** and support payroll-related processes.
* Ensure proper **onboarding and orientation** of new hires.
* Act as the primary point of contact for employee queries, coordination issues, and internal communication.
* Support management in implementing **HR policies, discipline, and professional conduct guidelines**.
* Assist in **performance tracking, feedback collection, and appraisal coordination**.
* Help maintain a positive, professional, and productive work culture.

---

## **Marketing & Brand Management Responsibilities**

* Oversee, update, and maintain the **company website**, including content updates and coordination with technical teams when required.
* Manage and regularly update **LinkedIn and other social media platforms** to ensure consistent branding and communication.
* Plan and execute **digital marketing activities**, including posts, announcements, hiring campaigns, and product updates.
* Create or coordinate creation of basic marketing materials such as:

  * Social media creatives
  * Company presentations
  * Brochures
  * Pitch decks
  * Case studies
  * Website content
* Support **lead generation efforts** through LinkedIn outreach, email campaigns, and other digital channels.
* Track engagement metrics and prepare **basic weekly or monthly marketing reports**.
* Help position the company as an **AI-powered software and services organization** through consistent messaging.
* Coordinate with founders and technical teams to understand offerings and communicate them clearly to clients and prospects.

---

## **Client & External Coordination Responsibilities**

* Maintain **professional relationships with clients, partners, and external companies**.
* Assist in client communication, follow-ups, scheduling meetings, and coordination.
* Support preparation of **proposals, introductory emails, and client communication drafts**.
* Ensure timely follow-ups with leads, prospects, and collaborators.

---

## **Administrative & Operational Support**

* Maintain structured records for employees, freelancers, contracts, and client documents.
* Support coordination between management, technical teams, HR, and external stakeholders.
* Assist in scheduling meetings, interviews, reviews, and internal discussions.
* Ensure smooth execution of administrative and operational workflows.
* Support management in any additional coordination or operational tasks required for company growth.

---

*This role requires a proactive, organized individual capable of handling HR, marketing, coordination, and basic administrative responsibilities in a growing software and AI-driven organization.*
